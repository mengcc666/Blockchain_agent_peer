Flow:

[1] Download fabric (*mind the version)

[2] Download fabric-samples

[3] Find "releases" in github.com/hyperledger/fabirc and download corresponding version(fabric version in Flow[1]) of file which contains "bin" folder

[4] Move files in "bin" in Flow[3] to bin in fabric-samples

[5] Open "fabric/scripts/bootstrap.sh", scroll down, set "DOCKER" and "SAMPLES" with value false

[6] > ./bootstrap.sh (this step downloads the docker)

[7] > cd fabric-samples/test-network
    > ./network.sh up
